QABELI

Ingredients

  1. Basmati rice -- 3 cups
  2. Oil -- 1/2 cup
  3. Stewing lamb or beef, cubed -- 2 pounds
  4. Onions, thinly sliced -- 2
  5. Garlic, minced -- 3 or 4
  6. Cinnamon -- 1 stick
  7. Cardamom pods -- 8 to 10
  8. Cumin seeds -- 2 teaspoons
  9. Whole cloves -- 6 to 8
  10. Stock or water -- 2 or 3 cups
  11. Salt and pepper -- to taste
  12. Carrots, peeled and cut into matchsticks or shredded -- 3
  13. Sugar -- 1/4 cup
  14. Raisins, soaked in water and drained -- 1/2 cup
  15. Salt -- to taste
  16. Sliced almonds, toasted -- 1/2 cup
  17. Garam masala -- 2 teaspoons

Methods

  1. boil beef in water adding all ground spices and salt till tender.it wil take half an hour in cooker.
  2. take oil in pan add onion and fry it til got brown.now add tomatoes in it.
  3. add beed and fry it for 5 to 10 minutes
  4. now add stew (yakhni) which should be double of the rice
  5. add salt,all ground spices, raisin
  6. when some water is left add all the nuts in it and then cover it for dam for 20 minutes.
  7. cut carrots into thin slices boil it for 5 minutes thn drain water
  8. add 1 tbp oil in pan thn add boied carrots in it and sugar sot it for a while then remove from stove.
  9. put rice into serving dish put these carrots on it and also fine choped nuts for garnishing.
